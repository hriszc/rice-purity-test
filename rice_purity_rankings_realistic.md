### Methodology: Latent Variable Simulation
This model accounts for the high correlation between behaviors. We used a latent factor model with correlation **rho=0.85**. In this realistic model, people tend to progress through experiences in a somewhat ordered fashion (e.g., kissing leads to more intense activities). The previous 'independent' model resulted in scores unnaturally clustered around the mean (SD ~3); this new model shows a much more realistic spread (SD ~20+).
